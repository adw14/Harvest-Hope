About Harvesting Hope

Harvesting Hope is a program dedicated to empowering teenagers aged 13-18 to tackle food inequality and embrace sustainable farming practices. Our multifaceted curriculum encompasses education, skill development, community engagement, and advocacy. We focus on understanding the root causes of food inequality and the importance of access to nutritious food.

Hands-on Activities

Participants engage in various hands-on activities including organic gardening, community garden projects, and volunteering at food banks.
